What we measure

Your cognitive processing speed, emotional regulation, and decision accuracy under simulated high-stakes pressure, whether your performance elevates, holds, or degrades when the game is on the line.

How it's scored

A single number from 0 to 1000, benchmarked against thousands of athletes in your sport and age group. It's a percentile of mental performance, not a grade.

A note on your score

Clutch Factor is one piece of your athletic profile, not the whole story. A lower score doesn't mean you're a bad player; it means your physical skills are ahead of your mental ones right now. The good news: clutch is highly trainable.

Treat it as a baseline

Elite athletes train the mind like the body. Know your tier, find your limiter, and use targeted reps to push into the next bracket. Your score is a starting line, not a label.

Train it up

Your drills are pulled straight from your Clutch Factor and NTerpret reports. Run them between sessions, then re-test in your next window to see the number move.

Own the conversation

A verified score is something coaches can't get anywhere else. Use it to show you've done the mental work, then back it up on the field.
