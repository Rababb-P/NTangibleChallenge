What we measure

Your cognitive processing speed, emotional regulation, and decision accuracy under simulated high-stakes pressure, whether your performance elevates, holds, or degrades when the game is on the line.

How to use this

Treat your score as a baseline. Elite athletes train the mind like the body. Know your tier, find your limiter, and use targeted reps to push into the next bracket.

A note on your score

Clutch Factor is one piece of your athletic profile, not the whole story. A lower score doesn't mean you're a bad player; it means your physical skills are ahead of your mental ones right now. The good news: clutch is highly trainable. The reps below close the gap.
